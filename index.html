About Us

Our History

Founded in 2015, Altair Spaceworks started as a small club of space enthusiasts at our university. Over the years, we've grown into a robust student union that's made significant contributions to amateur rocketry. We have successfully launched numerous rockets, each one more advanced than the last, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in amateur rocketry. We're proud to have sparked an interest in space exploration across the university and beyond.

Our Mission

At Altair Spaceworks, our mission is to expand knowledge of rocketry and space exploration among the student body. We strive to inspire the next generation of scientists, engineers, and innovators by giving them hands-on experience in designing, building, and launching rockets. We also aim to collaborate with industry professionals to provide our members with unique learning opportunities. Our vision is a world where everyone has the opportunity to contribute to the exploration of space.

Meet the Team

Our team consists of students from various disciplines who share a common interest in space exploration and rocket science. Our diverse backgrounds and perspectives fuel our innovative approach to rocketry. With a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills, we are constantly pushing the envelope, exploring new ideas, and challenging the status quo in rocket design and space exploration. Come meet the dedicated individuals who are making a difference at Altair Spaceworks.
