About Us

Altair Spaceworks is a passionate student union dedicated to the field of rocketry. Our team consists of students from various disciplines who share a common interest in space exploration and rocket science. Together, we work on designing, building, and launching rockets while promoting STEM education and fostering a collaborative environment.
